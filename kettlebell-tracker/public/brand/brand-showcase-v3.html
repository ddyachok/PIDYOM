06 // RESEARCH FOUNDATION

WHY EVERYTHING MEANS SOMETHING

THE NAME

PIDYOM (Підйом) — Ukrainian for "lift, rise, ascent." Military reveille command meaning "Get up!" Hebrew root P-D-Y (פדיון) means "redemption." Combined: Liberation through lifting.

THE SHAPE

Circle = the rock, the original training tool. Humanity's first expression of strength was picking up a stone. Arc = the swing path, grip, rising trajectory. 5° tilt = dynamic tension, always in motion. All ratios from Fibonacci sequence → golden proportion.

THE COLOR

Charcoal = cast iron (kettlebells are raw iron). Chalk = lifting chalk, limestone, pale granite. Volt = bioluminescence, new growth, spring energy. Not trend colors — material reality of the training environment.

THE POSITION

PIDYOM occupies a space no competitor does: heritage tool + modern approach. Not Peloton (too corporate), not CrossFit (too aggressive), not Whoop (too clinical). PIDYOM is ancient energy tracked with modern precision.
