## The Core Symbol: "The Rising Arc"

### Concept
A single, continuous arc that reads as:
1. A **kettlebell handle** being lifted
2. A **golden spiral segment** (mathematical growth)
3. The Ukrainian letter form hidden in the negative space
4. An **upward trajectory** вҖ” the path of something being picked up

### The Shape Language

The mark combines 3 geometric primitives:
1. **Circle** (body) = completeness, weight, the rock, the kettlebell body
2. **Arc** (handle) = motion, trajectory, grip, the swing path
3. **Upward tilt** = the entire mark tilts ~8В° from vertical = dynamic tension, about to move

The 8В° tilt is derived from the **golden angle**: 137.5В° Г· 17 вүҲ 8В°, creating subtle dynamic energy without feeling "fallen over."

### Three Versions

#### A. Full Logo (Detailed)
- The complete mark + "PIDYOM" wordmark
- Includes metadata elements (est. 2025, KETTLEBELL TRACKER)
- Used at: 200px+ width
- Grid-based editorial layout inspired by HIT reference

#### B. Simplified Icon (16Г—16)
- Reduces to: **circle + arc** only
- At 16Г—16: approximately 4-5px circle + 3px arc line
- Reads as: a weight with a handle / a letter P rotated
- Distinctive silhouette even at smallest sizes
- No wordmark, no details вҖ” pure geometric mark

#### C. Adaptive Color Variations
- **On dark** (#1A1A1A): Mark in Chalk White (#F0EDE8) or Volt (#C6FF00)
- **On light** (#F0EDE8): Mark in Charcoal (#1A1A1A)
- **Monotone**: Single color on any background
- **Volt accent**: Mark in charcoal with volt accent line (specific element highlighted)
