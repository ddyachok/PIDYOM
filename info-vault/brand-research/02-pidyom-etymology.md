# PIDYOM — Etymology & Meaning Research

## Hebrew Connection (פדיום / פִּדְיוֹם)
- **Pidyon** (פדיון) in Hebrew means **"redemption"** or **"ransom"** — the act of freeing something/someone
- **Pidyon HaBen** — ancient Jewish ceremony of "redeeming" the firstborn son
- Root: **P-D-Y** (פ-ד-י) — to redeem, to rescue, to set free
- "PIDYOM" could be read as a form of this root — **"the act of redemption"**
- This connects powerfully to fitness: **redeeming your body, freeing yourself through movement**

## Ukrainian / Slavic Angle
- **Підйом** (Pidyom) in Ukrainian literally means **"LIFT" / "RISE" / "ASCENT"**
- Breakdown: **під** (pid = up/under) + **йом** (yom = a rising movement)
- Used in contexts:
  - "Підйом!" — military command meaning **"Rise! Get up!"** (reveille)
  - "Підйом на гору" — ascent of a mountain
  - "Підйом ваги" — weight lift
  - "На підйомі" — on the rise, gaining momentum
  - "Легкий на підйом" — someone who is quick to get moving, ready for anything
- This is DIRECTLY connected to the brand values: **lift, get up, rise, move**

## The Word as Brand
- "PIDYOM" contains both meanings simultaneously:
  1. 🇺🇦 Ukrainian: **The physical act of lifting/rising**
  2. 🇮🇱 Hebrew: **Redemption/liberation**
- Combined meaning: **"Liberation through lifting"** or **"Rising as redemption"**
- The word itself encodes the brand philosophy: **free yourself by moving, redeem yourself through the primal act of picking up weight**

## Phonetic Analysis
- PI-DY-OM: 3 syllables, strong rhythm
- Starts with a **plosive P** (powerful, explosive)
- Contains **DY** (dynamic, kinetic energy)
- Ends with **OM** (completeness, resonance — also connects to meditative/mindful movement)
- The sound pattern mirrors the kettlebell swing: **explosive start (P) → dynamic arc (DY) → resonant completion (OM)**
