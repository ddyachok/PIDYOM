# PIDYOM — Brand Research Index

## What is PIDYOM?
A kettlebell training tracker and movement community.

**PIDYOM** (Підйом) = Ukrainian for "lift, rise, ascent" + Hebrew root for "redemption, liberation"

**Core meaning**: Liberation through lifting. Rise by picking up.

## Brand Essence (One Sentence)
**PIDYOM is the primal act of picking up a rock, refined into a practice, tracked with precision.**

## Brand Values
1. **Simplicity** — One tool, infinite movement. The kettlebell IS the gym
2. **Flow** — Not reps and sets, but continuous, rhythmic, beautiful movement
3. **Heritage** — 300+ years of kettlebell culture, 10,000+ years of stone lifting
4. **Progress** — Measurable, data-driven, trackable results
5. **Community** — Tribe of lifters, not followers of a brand
